About Project

sspeterpaulcathedral.com served as the official digital platform for Saints Peter and Paul Cathedral, Jabalpur. The website was designed as a fully dynamic web application with complete CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality, enabling seamless content management and parish operations.

The system was structured into three core modules:

  1. Public Portal

    The publicly accessible section provided essential information for visitors and parishioners, including:

    • Photo and video gallery
    • Notices and announcements
    • Mass timings
    • About the Church
    • Feedback and contact features
    This ensured clear communication and up-to-date information for the wider community.

  2. Parishioner Dashboard

    A secure, login-based portal for registered parishioners, offering:

    • Access to parish notices
    • Online payments and contributions
    • Mass bookings and related services
    • Personalized account management
    This module streamlined parish services and improved accessibility for members.

  3. Admin Dashboard

    A role-based administrative panel with full content and system management capabilities, including:

    • Adding, updating, and deleting notices
    • Managing photos, videos, and documents
    • Configuring payment categories
    • Updating “About Church” content
    • Overseeing parishioner interactions
    The admin interface enabled efficient backend management and real-time content updates without requiring technical intervention.
